The History of Takairim

The events of our campaign take part in the 7th era of the world. The Age of Ascendance.
The histories before the this age are lost to mortals, only the gods know what truly happened back then.
However, they have given us access to some information about the past.
If you cant find the information you are looking for, please beg the DM to elaborate, though be cautioned that some information is not meant for mortals.

Eras Description
The First Dawn (600-800 years) The Gods arrive and make the first mortals. The Gods make 3 Planes of existence (The Material Plane, the Fey wild and Faramirr)
The Elemental Cataclysm (300–400 years) The rise of the Primordials and the subsquent war between them and the Gods + mortals. The elemental planes are created with elemental creatures following.
The Age of Harmony (2,000–2,300 years) Mortals and Gods live together peacefully. More creatures are made. The Dread Pantheon begin to turn. Creation of the first monsters.
The First Heavenly War (2,300–2,450 years) The first war between The Celestial Court and The Dread Lords. The Hells are created with other races and monsters. Magic is gifted to Mortals. The Holy Blockade is cast.
The Age of Sorcery (2,450–2,650 years) The development of magic used by Mortals. No restrictions on the magic used. The Arcane Syndicate gains power and break The Holy Blockade releaseing The Dread Lords.
The Second Heavenly War (2,650–2,750 years) The second fight between The Celestial Court and The Dread Lords. The abyss, pandemonium and other planes are created with other monsters and races. The outer planes are created with the second casting of The Holy Blockade
The Age of Ascendance (0AB-Present) Current. Mortals adjust to life without the Gods direct influence. Greedy, power-hungry mortals threaten the world multiple times, during the rise and fall of many Kingdoms and Empires
