The History of Takairim

The events of our campaign take part in the 7th era of the world.

See the side for more information on each era.

Eras Description
The First Dawn The Gods arrive and make the first mortals. The Gods make 3 Planes of existence (The Material Plane, the Fey wild and Faramirr)
The Primal Strife The rise of the Primordials and the subsquent war between them and the Gods + mortals. The elemental planes are created with elemental creatures following.
The Age of Tranquility Mortals and Gods live together peacefully. More creatures are made. The Dread Pantheon begin to turn. Creation of the first monsters.
The First Heavenly War The first war between The Celestial Court and The Dread Lords. The Hells are created with other races and monsters. Magic is gifted to Mortals. The Holy Blockade is cast.
The Age of Magi The development of magic used by Mortals. No restrictions on the magic used. The Arcane Syndicate gains power and break The Holy Blockade releaseing The Dread Lords.
The Second Heavenly War The second fight between The Celestial Court and The Dread Lords. The abyss, pandemonium and other planes are created with other monsters and races. The outer planes are created with the second casting of The Holy Blockade
The Age of Adjustment Current. Mortals adjust to life without the Gods direct influence. Greedy, power-hungry mortals threaten the world multiple times.
